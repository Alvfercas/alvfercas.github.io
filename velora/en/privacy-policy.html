Privacy Policy of Velora

Last Updated: 03/04/2025

At Velora, we respect your privacy and strive to be transparent about how we handle any type of information within our application. Below, we outline our privacy policy, which applies to the use of Velora, an app designed for IPTV content playback using Xtream Codes credentials.

1. Information We Collect

Velora does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable user information. The app does not require registration or access to private data such as location, email, or contacts.

2. Use of Xtream Codes Credentials

To access IPTV content, users must enter their own Xtream Codes credentials. These credentials are stored locally on the device and are neither sent nor collected by Velora. The security and use of these credentials are the sole responsibility of the user.

3. Third-Party Content

Velora is an IPTV player and does not provide or host any multimedia content. All content accessible through the app is managed by external IPTV providers with whom users have an active subscription. Velora has no control over the legality, availability, or quality of content offered by third parties.

4. Donations

Velora allows users to make voluntary donations to support the development of the application through PayPal and Buy Me a Coffee.

When accessing the donation links, users will be redirected to their respective payment platforms, where the transaction is securely processed. Velora does not collect, process, or store any financial or personal information related to donations.

For more details on how these platforms handle your data, you can review their privacy policies:

  • PayPal Privacy Policy
  • Buy Me a Coffee Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

Velora is committed to protecting user privacy. Since we do not collect personal information or store sensitive data on external servers, the security risks are minimal. We do not share or sell any user data to third parties.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app or our procedures. Any relevant modifications will be published on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed of any updates.
